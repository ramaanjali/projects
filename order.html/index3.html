Varieties Of Dosa

Plain Dosa

Every restaurant can serve this type of dosa. This dosa is soft textured and has the crispness in it. The dosa generally made can be thin or thick.

₹250

Masala Dosa

It is a unique spicy, crispy, and sweet dosa having a filling of delicious potato masala.

₹180

Instant Rava Dosa

This dosa can be made plain or one can make it more yummy or tangy by adding onions, chilies, and tomatoes.

₹250

Neer Dosa

his dosa can be served with sambhar, coconut chutney, peanut chutney, and veg-sagu.

₹250

Ragi Dosa

This dosa needs fermentation and hence is made like plain dosa. This dosa batter can be used in making idli and uttapam.

₹320

Cheese Dosa

This dosa is simple to make as this just require dosa batter and cheese. One can make it more interesting by adding vegetables, herbs, etc.

₹150
